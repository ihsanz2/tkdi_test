About Us

PT TKDI is a global Cloud Communication and IT services company incorporated in 2017. Our vision is to simplify communication for all. We have helped many enterprises achieve great results with minimal investment.


Our vision does not end there. We strive to help travel enthusiasts, holiday-goers and individuals like you and me to enjoy the same benefits.


With that, we are excited to present HolidaySIMcards. Simplicity has always been our philosophy. So, insert one of our sim cards into your phone on your next Holiday, and surf away!
